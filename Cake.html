VANILLA CAKE RECIPE

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 cups All-purpose flour
  2. 1 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  3. 1/4 tsp Salt
  4. 1/2 cup (1 stick) Unsalted butter, softened
  5. 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  6. 1/2 cup Milk, room temperature

Equipments:

  1. 2 (8-inch) round cake pans
  2. Mixing bowls
  3. Electric mixer (handheld or stand)
  4. Spatula
  5. Wire rack

Steps:

  1. Prep : Preheat oven to 175 degree celsius.
  2. Dry Mix : Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl.
  3. Cream : In a large bowl, beat softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy (2-3 mins).
  4. Add Eggs & Vanilla Essence : Beat one egg at a time, then add vanilla essence and mix.
  5. Combine : Gradually add dry mix and milk alternately to wet ingredients, starting and ending with dry. Mix on low speed until just combined. Do not overmix!
  6. Bake : Divide batter evenly into pans. Bake 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool : Cool in pans on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Then, invert both the cakes onto the wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Frost & Serve : Once fully cool, frost and enjoy!

HAPPY BAKING!
