Welcome To My Literary Corner!


    Percy Jackson & The Olympians Series By Rick Riordan

      Demigods, Greek mythology, and thrilling quests – what's not to love? Percy Jackson's world is full of action, humor, and heart. It's a fantastic adventure that truly brings ancient myths to life!

    Harry Potter Series By J.K. Rowling

      Stepping into the wizarding world is always an incredible escape. The magic, the friendships, and the epic battles make this series timeless. I love how deeply you connect with the characters and their journey at Hogwarts.

    The Diary Of A Young Girl By Anne Frank

      A powerful and incredibly moving account of a young girl's life during challenging times. Anne's voice is unforgettable, offering profound insights into hope, resilience, and the human spirit even in the darkest circumstances.

    The Book Thief By Markus Zusak

      Narrated by Death itself, this story set during WWII is a beautifully written, heartbreaking, and ultimately hopeful tale about the power of words, courage, and unexpected kindness in a world of turmoil. A truly profound read.

    The Forbidden Book By Sacha Lamb

      The allure of a hidden secret and forbidden knowledge makes this book incredibly intriguing. I was drawn into its mysterious pages, eagerly uncovering every twist and turn. Perfect for anyone who loves a good enigma!

    Once Upon A Wardrobe By Patti Callahan Henry

      For anyone who loves the magic of Narnia, this book offers a beautiful, heartwarming glimpse into the inspiration behind one of fantasy's greatest worlds. It's a tale of discovery, wonder, and the stories that shape us.

    The Case Of The Candy Bandit By Archit Taneja

      A delightful mystery that had me hooked from the first page! I loved piecing together the clues and trying to figure out who the culprit was. It’s a real page-turner for anyone who loves a good puzzle.
